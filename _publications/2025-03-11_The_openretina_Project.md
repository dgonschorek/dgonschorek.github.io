Studying the retina plays a crucial role in understanding how the visual world is translated into the brain's language. This work presents openretina, an open-source Python/PyTorch platform that unifies retinal modelling efforts across species and methodologies, providing shared model architectures, data loaders, and tools for computational analysis and hypothesis testing in retinal neuroscience.
